— Our Story —

Crafted in Gold, Lived in Memory

Mari Gold Marquees was founded with a singular conviction — that the most precious moments of life deserve a setting equal to their meaning. From our home on Main GT Road, Islamabad, we have spent years quietly crafting events that families remember for generations.

A Legacy of Elegance

Every drape, every floral, every candle is placed with intention. Our team blends old-world craftsmanship with contemporary design to deliver experiences that feel both timeless and effortlessly modern.

Rooted in Islamabad

Opposite DHA-2 Gate-1, we sit at one of the capital's most accessible addresses — a venue that's earned its reputation through hundreds of flawlessly executed weddings, corporate galas and private celebrations.
